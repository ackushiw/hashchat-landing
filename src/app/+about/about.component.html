About Us

#WhatIsHashChat

Millions use hashtags daily to add clarity and character to their posts. So HashChat was made to let friends, fans, networks and customers use tags to interact around ideas.

Our free iOS app turns a hashtag into a custom URL for any discussion. Features include one-step posting, dynamic discovery, predictive search, private messaging and sharable links. But we have a lot more planned in the near future.

The app's design was inspired by iconic NYC MTA maps, because we like to think HashChat gets folks to their destination like transit does.

#WhoAreWe

We are former "madmen" who literally worked in digital advertising on Madison Avenue. Our Brooklyn-based team has created interactive work for some of the world's top brands. When we saw an opportunity to apply our experience to instant topic-based communities, we jumped on it.  

#OurVision

We believe in the power of group conversation to help, inform and entertain. There are a lot of great broadcast-based messaging apps, and we love to use them. However, HashChat's special goal is to connect people around ideas to make life easier and more fun.
